Kia Models 2019 Kia Rio

The Kia Rio is a subcompact car produced by the South Korean manufacturer Kia since August 2000 and now in its third generation. The first generation Rio was mostly marketed as the Kia Pride in export markets, excluding the United States and Canada, which received the Rio name. The Rio replaced the Kia Sephia, which was discontinued in 2000. The Rio was also marketed as the Kia Cinco in South Korea.

 2019 Kia Forte

The Kia Forte is a compact car manufactured by Kia Motors since 2009. It is available in sedan and hatchback body styles. The Forte is a replacement for the Kia Cerato, which was discontinued in 2010. The Forte is marketed globally, except in China, where it is known as the K3. The Forte is also marketed as the Kia Forte Koup, a two-door coupe version of the sedan, and the Kia Forte5, a five-door hatchback version of the sedan.

 2019 Kia Optima

The Kia Optima is a mid-size car manufactured by Kia Motors since 2000 and marketed globally through various nameplates. First generation cars were mostly marketed as the Optima, although the Kia Magentis name was used in Europe and Canada when sales began there in 2002. For the North American market the first generation Optima was marketed as the Optima, although the Kia Magentis name was used in Canada when sales began there in 2002. The second generation Optima, Kia Optima Hybrid, and Kia Optima Plug-In Hybrid were released in 2016. The Optima is Kia's mid-size sedan and is manufactured at Kia's South Korean facility in Namyang, Gwangmyeong, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea. The Optima is also available as a hybrid and a plug-in hybrid.

 2019 Kia Sorento

The Kia Sorento is a mid-size crossover SUV manufactured by Kia Motors since 2002. The first generation Sorento was a traditional body-on-frame SUV. The second generation Sorento was introduced in 2009 and is a unibody vehicle. The third generation Sorento was introduced in 2015. The Sorento is marketed as the Mohave in China and as the Borrego in the Middle East and North Africa. The Sorento is Kia's largest SUV and is manufactured at the company's South Korean facility in Sohari, Gwangju, South Korea. The Sorento is also available as a hybrid.
